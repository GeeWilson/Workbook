Annual Competency Workbook

As part of your recruitment process, Thornbury Nursing requires you to complete an online competency-based assessment. This covers standard requirements such as health & safety, child protection, infection control and medication calculations.

The Workbook takes the form of 98 questions, arranged over 7 sections. You'll be given 7 attempts to complete the Workbook, and you will need to answer all questions correctly in order to pass. If all attempts are used then you will be locked out of the system and will not be able to work for us, and we reserve the right to review your registration with us.

In order to avoid this we strongly advise you to refer to the reading material when the questions are presented to help you answer the questions. If you get any wrong you will then be given another chance to correct just those answers (up to a maximum of 7 attempts).

Our records show that you are a Theatre Nurse. If this is correct then you do not need to take any further action, and you are free to begin completing your Workbook.

If this is incorrect, please update your qualification below and select the “Update qualification” button to update your records. This update will be checked by our team and you will be contacted once this process is complete.
